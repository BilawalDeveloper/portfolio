Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple LLM integration might take 1-2 weeks, while a full RAG system implementation could take 4-8 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase.

Do you work with startups or only enterprises?

I work with both! Whether you're a startup building your MVP or an enterprise scaling AI systems, I adapt my approach to your needs and budget. I offer flexible engagement models for different company sizes.

What technologies and frameworks do you use?

I specialize in Python (FastAPI, Django), Node.js (Express, NestJS), LangChain, LlamaIndex, OpenAI, Anthropic, vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, ChromaDB), and modern deployment tools (Docker, AWS, Vercel). I choose the best stack for your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes! All projects include 30 days of post-launch support. After that, I offer monthly retainer packages for ongoing maintenance, updates, and optimization. I also provide emergency support for critical issues.

Can you help with existing AI projects?

Absolutely! I offer code reviews, architecture audits, performance optimization, and can help rescue struggling projects. Many clients come to me to improve or scale their existing AI systems.

What's included in the project cost?

Project costs include development, testing, deployment, documentation, and initial support. Third-party costs (API keys, hosting, databases) are billed separately at cost. No hidden fees - everything is transparent upfront.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out through the contact form below or email me directly. We'll schedule a free 30-minute discovery call to discuss your project. No commitment required for the initial conversation!
